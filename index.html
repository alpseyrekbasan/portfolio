About

Hello! My name is Alp. I am a multidisciplinary artist and designer working with sound, video, and code. I create digital tools, build websites, and design posters & album covers. Head over to the works section for a quick demo of my creative coding projects or check out my instgram account graphic.basan for my design works. Other projects such as video and sound art can be found on my soundcloud and vimeo pages. If you have any questions or want to get in touch with me feel free to hit me up through the channels written in the contact section of this website.
